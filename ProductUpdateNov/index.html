NimbleDroid Product Update, Nov 2016
 

Welcome to the November NimbleDroid newsletter. We have tons of new features and enhancements to go over, so let’s get started.

 
Alerts for slowness, memory leaks, and crashes
 
If you have NimbleDroid integrated with your Continuous Integration server for continuous analysis (which we highly recommend!), we have some good news. We now support email and Slack alerts that you can configure to inform you about slowdowns, memory leaks, or app crashes. Stop manually checking your results and automate your way to peace of mind!
 
You can configure Alerts for your app in the Settings/Channels and Settings/Alerts tabs. Support for Alerts via webhook is also on the horizon.
 
Review performance for all uploads in one graph
 
We’ve heard from users that they want to really dive into app performance trending, not just see the last 5 uploads. We happy to introduce expanded graph capabilities, including the ability to see your entire performance history on one graph.
 
 
New pricing and discounts until December 15
 
Over a year after our initial beta launch, we are introducing tiered pricing plans to NimbleDroid. Plans range from Team all the way up to Enterprise. Some of our newest and most powerful features are only available with one of our paid plans, so be sure to check it out. And from now until December 15, we are offering discounted prices on our plans. You can reserve your discounted price without being charged yet, so be sure to act soon.
 
 
See NimbleDroid features and plans
 
 
Enhancements to custom user flows
 
For our premium plan customers using custom Scenarios defined in Espresso, we expanded your profiling capabilities to allow you to get as granular and comprehensive as possible. Profile “bookends” that specify exact points in your code you want profiled can now have IDs, allowing you to create multiple profiles per test case, nesting of bookends in your code, and the ability to start or end bookends in either test or app code.
 
If you’d like to hear more about this feature, let us know!
 
Great time at droidcon London
 
We enjoyed attending droidcon London, our first conference outside of the United States. The energy there was very high and equalled that of the best conferences we’ve attended. We had two winners of our App Garage Performance Clinic, Track My Shows by DeltaLabs and GLAMI by Inspigroup.com. Congratulations!
 
That’s all for this month. If you have any questions about what you’ve seen here, feel free to reach out at contact@nimbledroid.com
