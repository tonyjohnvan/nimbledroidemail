NimbleDroid Product Update, May 2016
 

This month we have been primarily focused on improving some of the unseen parts of NimbleDroid, as well as laying the groundwork for some exciting features coming next month. Here are some updates that you will be able to see now.

Better Management of Uploads

We have a number of users now who have been doing automated Gradle uploads for months, or who might have uploaded some “test” versions of an app when evaluating NimbleDroid. Some of these users wanted to remove certain specific uploads from their results. We have added the ability to do so under the Uploads tab on the App page...

Better Breakdown of Method Count

Our previous Method Count screen provided some good insight into what SDKs were eating up the non-MultiDex limit. Unfortunately, the way we conveyed the information and handled certain unknown SDKs caused confusion. Because of feedback on this we have re-worked the Method Count screen...
