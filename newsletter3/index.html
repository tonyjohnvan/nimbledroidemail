Why Is ClassLoader .getResourceAsStream So Slow in Android?
 
The method ClassLoader.getResourceAsStream is pretty popular in the Java world, but unfortunately it causes gigantic slowdown in an Android app the first time it is invoked. Of all the apps and SDKs we’ve analyzed (and we’ve analyzed a ton), we’ve seen that over 10% of apps and 20% of SDKs are slowed down significantly by this method. What exactly is going on?
